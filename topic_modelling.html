Project Description

Objective

The aim of this project is to create a model to categorise news headlines into distinct topics. This dataset, taken from Kaggle, contains a total of 73537 unique news headlines taken from the Reddit World News channel.

Methodology

To begin, I performed text processing on the news headlines, which includes removing punctuation as well as lemmatizing. Lemmatizing is the process where words are being reduced to their root form. For instance, 'playing' would be reduced to 'play'. After lemmatizing, I created a document term matrix, which is a mathematical matrix that describes the frequency of keywords that occur in a collection of documents. Rows in the matrix correspond to documents in the collection, while columns correspond to the keyword. In our case, rows correspond to the news headlines.

Next, I applied Non-Negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) on the document term matrix. This is a statistical method that helps to reduce the dimension of the input corpora. The output of NMF is another matrix, where each row represents a specific topic, and the columns representing the keywords in each topic.

Conclusion

After tuning the parameters of the NMF model, I found that the news headlines are best categroised into 10 distinct topics.
